# Civitas — Trust Check for Verified Humans

> World proves who you are.  
> Civitas helps determine whether you should trust them.

**Before you trust, check the pattern.**

Civitas is a **trust infrastructure layer** that turns real-world interactions into structured, evidence-backed signals — helping people and systems make decisions *before* money, work, or reputation is at risk.

---

## 🧠 The Problem

Across hiring, lending, freelance work, and collaborations, the same failure repeats:

- Payments get delayed  
- Commitments break  
- Communication disappears  

But before every decision, everything looks fine:
- profiles  
- resumes  
- references  

Because **behavior is invisible at the moment of decision**.

This creates three systemic gaps:

- **Trust without institutions** → no portable reputation  
- **Verification without managers** → no reliable version of truth  
- **Disputes without courts** → no structured path to resolution  

---

## ⚡ Why Now

As AI agents and global coordination scale:

- execution is becoming cheap  
- trust is becoming expensive  
- verification is becoming the bottleneck  

Systems can act faster than humans can audit.

> **Scale without verification becomes a liability.**

Civitas makes trust **computable** — so decisions can be made before risk is taken.

---

## 🚀 The Solution

Civitas creates **structured, verifiable trust signals** from real interactions.

Instead of opinions or ratings, Civitas captures:

- what was expected  
- what actually happened  
- evidence and context  
- responses from both sides  
- corroborations over time  

These inputs become **pattern-based signals** that guide decision-making.

---

## 🔍 How Civitas Works

### 1. Trust Check  
**See behavior before you commit.**

Search a person or ai agents to view structured trust signals.

#### Example Trust Snapshot

---

### 2. Record Interaction  
**Turn real interactions into structured records.**

- Relationship context (lending, ai work delivery, hiring, landlord, freelance, etc.)  
- Expected vs actual outcome  
- Timeline  
- Evidence (documents, messages, links)  

---

### 3. Response Layer  
**Capture both sides of the story.**

Participants can:
- acknowledge  
- dispute  
- clarify  
- resolve  

Creating a **bidirectional record of truth**

---

### 4. Pattern Detection  
**Signals emerge from repeated behavior.**

A single incident doesn’t define trust.

> People don’t fail once. They repeat.

---

## 🧠 What Makes Civitas Different

- Not a review platform  
- Not a rating system  
- Not a court  

Civitas is a **structured truth layer** that enables:

- trust without institutions  
- verification without managers  
- disputes without courts

## 🌐 World Integration

Civitas is built as a **World Mini App**, extending identity into trust.

- **World ID (staging/simulator)** → proves user is human  
- **Wallet Auth** → identity-linked interaction  
- **Civitas** → behavioral trust layer  

> World proves who you are.  
> Civitas helps determine whether you should trust them.

Together:
> **Identity → Interaction → Verification → Trust**

---

## 🤖 Beyond Humans

The same system applies beyond humans.

As agents act and transact independently, trust shifts from identity to execution.

Without verifiable behavior, agent systems become opaque and risky.

Civitas enables:

- Execution reliability tracking  
- Response consistency  
- Failure and correction history  
- Accountability over time  

> Civitas creates a shared trust layer across humans and agents.
